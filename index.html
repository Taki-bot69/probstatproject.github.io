BLACKJACK

How to Play:

1. The game starts with the dealer dealing two cards to each player, including themselves. One of the dealer's cards is face down, while the other is face up.

2. The goal of the game is to get a hand value of 21 or as close to 21 as possible without going over.

3. The value of each card is as follows: Ace is worth 1 or 11 points, face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10 points, and all other cards are worth their face value.

4. After receiving their initial two cards, each player can choose to "hit" and receive additional cards, or "stand" and keep their current hand.

5. If a player's hand value exceeds 21, they "bust" and lose the game. If the dealer busts, all remaining players win.

6. Once all players have taken their turns, the dealer reveals their face-down card and must hit until their hand value is at least 17.

7. If the dealer's hand value exceeds 21, all remaining players win. Otherwise, the dealer compares their hand to each player's hand and pays out any winnings.

8. In some variations of the game, players can also choose to "double down" and receive only one additional card after doubling their initial bet, or "split" their hand if they receive two cards of the same value.

9. The game continues until all players have finished their turns, or until the deck is depleted.

10. Remember to enjoy the Game GOODLUCK!!
